A future with Valor Software

Do you want to get involved in fun and challenging projects that also allow you to use your favorite technologies? We're looking for new people to join the Valor family!

What we can offer

Flat Structure

everyone can turn to everyone in the company, minimum bureaucracy, interdependencies, and formalities, communication is the key

Regular compensation review

when you feel like your compensation needs to be reconsidered, you just make a corresponding request, no stress

Remote

even though we have a 4-flour office after renovation, everyone can work from home, constantly, or from time to time

Recreation points

you’ll have access to a game room, a massage room, and a huge open terrace on the fourth floor with a view of the old city of Kharkiv

Pet-friendly

we had been pet-friendly long before it became mainstream. Valor hosts three cats, and we’re open if you’ll come over with your friendly pet to accompany us at work

Comfort

you’ll find all you need and even more in the office, starting from treats like drinks and snacks, up to medicine, literature, people’s support, and cozy chairs :)

Art

for those who appreciate drawing, photography, or memes, we’ll have a whole floor decorated with works of Valorians, join, and share your art

English native speakers

we have native speakers on board, some of them reside in Ukraine, and some – in the US, anyway, you can talk to them a lot on English classes or in your spare time

Culture of personal growth

we’re not crazy about being the best version of ourselves, but we recognize the necessity of moving to stay up to date, and we’ll help you uncover your best qualities

Open source

while everyone is mostly taking from open source, we think it’s time to give back. We maintain and contribute to various popular open-source projects, and you can too

GDE on board

CEO Dmitriy Shekhovtsov is the first Angular Google Developer Expert in Ukraine, and he is also the author of the ngx-bootstrap library

Angular community activists

Valor is a JavaScript-focused company formed by the developer for the developers. We contribute to open source and organized the first Angular conference in Ukraine ->ngTalks.io

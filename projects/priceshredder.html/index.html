Priceshredder

Priceshredder helps people who are already insured by the company to change the conditions of insurance at a more affordable price with the help of insurance brokers of another company.

Goals

1

Add notification and modal popups support

2

Create a design for the Buyer profile and Seller/Buyer Dashboard

3

Add support of themes

4

Redesign the platform and implement Blog functionality

Solutions

1

We provided support for multi-user accounts and implemented WebSockets for this goal

2

Integrated the Stripe payment system

Outcome

The useful and popular smartphone-based platform that helps insurance buyers receive and manage bids from major insurance companies. As a result, PriceShredder allows an easier, faster, and more efficient way to shop for insurance.

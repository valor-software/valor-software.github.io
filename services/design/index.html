Design

Designing solutions with users in mind, we strive to make their journey an intuitive and breathtaking adventure. Therefore before proceeding with UI, we help you define the frustrations of your audience and cover them with optimal UX.

Services

UX Audit

We’ll carefully analyze your product to pinpoint less than perfect areas, then – develop a step-by-step improvement plan. UX audit suggests quick-win changes that optimize user journeys and fuel customers’ loyalty.

Business objectives checkup

From a series of interviews with stakeholders, we get a comprehensive view of present business goals and ongoing issues that stand in the way of growth.

Conducting user survey

Valor’s design team will take a deep dive into the customer experience and goals to discover the hidden potential for improvement through online surveys and A/B testing.

Gathering user analytics

With the help of Google Analytics and other tools, we’ll analyze how customers move through your platform and see at what stage of the journey users tend to drop off.

Usability checkup

Finally, we want to make sure your product keeps up with the UX best practices and usability heuristics. If the icons’ are not obvious, or the website navigation is tricky, let’s fix it!

A report and an action plan

We’ll conveniently visualize audit results for you using sitemaps, wireframes, and user flows. You’ll get valuable insights and a list of actions you should take to skyrocket UX.

Valor’s Traits

Ahead of design trends

The digital design industry is constantly evolving, and Valor’s design team monitors dynamics and keeps up to the pace. Being passionate about UX and UI, they take courses and participate in dedicated conferences. So when you partner up with Valor, you have the biggest fans of design working for your product and your audience. Visit a post-graduation case study of one of the designers to learn more.

Mature delivery process

We strive to make our collaboration with the clients transparent and predictable. So we have a particular flow for every stage when we iterate with you on team syncs, give progress updates, and make demos. This way, you always know where your project is now, what's coming next, and control the result. Learn more about the process of building solutions from scratch from the Startups dedicated page.

Knowledge exchange

We have one of the most close-knit and well-coordinated design teams you can ever imagine. Folks made experience exchange part of their schedule with regular sessions when each presents on a hot topic. Embracing curiosity and collective knowledge, we can find more efficient solutions to your business challenges.
